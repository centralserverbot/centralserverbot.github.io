About Central

Welcome to Central, your premier ER:LC private server management tool. Established in January 2024, Central has rapidly expanded, managing over 1,000 servers within the first four months, over 2,000 servers in the first six months, and over 3,000 servers in the first 7 months.

Our community's excitement keeps us motivated to continuously improve and add new features. At Central, we're dedicated to providing the best ER:LC bot experience for your Discord server.
